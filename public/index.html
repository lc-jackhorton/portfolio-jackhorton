    Projects

    LC Circuit

    For my Experimental Physics Class, I'm doing a project where using a solenoid I create a magnetic field. That magnetic field causes the electric field flowing through a capacitor to flip back and forth in the direction its flowing, this causes the magnetic field to also flip back and forth. In other words, the electric and magnetic field have a inverse sinusoidal relationship. I study that with a oscilloscope.

    LC Circuit Info

    Five Color Theorem

    For my Combinatorics Class, I'm doing a project where I prove that if you have a map of countries and want to color that map so that two two colors are adjacent to each other, you can do that with five colors no matter the shapes of the countries. I used LaTeX and Paint to create vector diagrams and to format the proof.
